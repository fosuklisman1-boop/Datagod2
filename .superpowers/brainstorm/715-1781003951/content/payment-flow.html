⚙️ Implementation: new WhatsApp-only step

  • Add WA_ENTER_PAYMENT_PHONE step to session type
  • In WhatsApp bot router: when USSD handler returns ussdServiceOp: 2 from PAYMENT_METHOD selecting MoMo → intercept and insert this new step instead
  • Handler validates phone format, stores as session.momoPhone, then calls the MoMo charge with that number
  • Applies to all 3 services: Data Bundles, Airtime, Results Checker
  • Wallet payment (option 1) is unchanged — no phone needed
